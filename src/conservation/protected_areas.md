# Protected Areas

## Major Reserves

**India**: Ranthambore, Kanha, Bandhavgarh, Kaziranga, Nagarhole, Sunderbans (50+ reserves)
**Russia**: Sikhote-Alin, Land of the Leopard
**Indonesia**: Gunung Leuser, Way Kambas, Ujung Kulon
**Thailand**: Thung Yala, Huai Kha Khaeng
**Malaysia**: Taman Negara, Endau-Rompin

## Coverage

~50 tiger reserves across range. Total area insufficient for minimum viable population in some subspecies.
