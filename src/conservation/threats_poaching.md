# Threats: Poaching

## Illegal Trade

Tigers killed for traditional medicine (bones for wine), decorative skins, and body parts. Black market prices rising as tigers become rarer.

## Demand Drivers

Traditional Asian medicine market. Collectors. International trafficking networks.

## Enforcement Challenges

Low prosecution rates. Corruption. Cross-border crime. Insufficient patrol resources.

## Countermeasures

- Anti-poaching patrol units
- Sniffer dog detection
- Community watch programs
- Public awareness campaigns
- Strict legal penalties
