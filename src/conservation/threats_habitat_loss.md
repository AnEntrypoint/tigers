# Threats: Habitat Loss

## Deforestation

Industrial agriculture, timber extraction, infrastructure development. Loss of forest cover eliminates stalking habitat and prey base.

## Fragmentation

Roads and human settlement divide tiger populations. Isolated groups face inbreeding. Dispersal blocked.

## Agricultural Expansion

Rice paddies, plantations, human settlement replace forest. Resource competition intensifies.

## Mitigation

Wildlife corridors connecting protected areas. Community-managed forests. Transboundary cooperation.
