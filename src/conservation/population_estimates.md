# Population Estimates

Approximately 3,500-5,000 individuals in the wild (2023). Down from 100,000 a century ago.

## Historical Decline

- 1900: ~100,000
- 1950: ~50,000
- 1970: ~5,000
- 2023: ~4,000

Most severe decline 1950-1980 due to poaching and habitat loss.
