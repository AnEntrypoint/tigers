# Tiger Mythology

## Hindu

Goddess Durga rides a tiger, symbolizing divine power over dangerous forces. Tigers in temple art.

## Chinese

One of 12 animals in zodiac. Brings prosperity (wealthsymbol). Year of the Tiger celebrated.

## Indigenous

Many cultures across range have tiger legends. Warning tales. Deification in some traditions.

## Negative

Demons may take tiger form in some folklore. Taboos about tigers in select cultures.
