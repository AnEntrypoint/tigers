# Indochinese Tiger (Panthera tigris corbetti)

## Description

Named after Jim Corbett. Found across mainland Southeast Asia.

## Physical Features

Medium size, darker orange than Bengal. Males weigh 150-195 kg. Stripes moderately wide.

## Population

Approximately 350 individuals across Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, Cambodia, Myanmar, southern China.

## Habitat

Tropical and subtropical forests, mountainous terrain. Prefers dense forest cover.

## Conservation

Fragmented across multiple countries with limited coordinated protection.
