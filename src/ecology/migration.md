# Movement Patterns

## Territorial Stability

Adults maintain year-round territories. Dispersing subadults seek new ranges.

## Seasonal Movements

- Siberian tigers move south slightly in winter toward prey concentrations
- Monsoon affects hunting patterns in tropical regions
- Altitudinal migration in some Himalayan populations

## Range Sizes

- Male home ranges: 20-150 km²
- Female home ranges: 10-40 km²
- Sizes correlate inversely with prey density
