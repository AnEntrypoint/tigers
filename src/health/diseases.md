# Diseases

## Canine Distemper

Affects big cats. Outbreaks documented in some populations. Vaccination programs in captive populations.

## Feline Diseases

Feline leukemia virus, feline immunodeficiency virus. Monitored in captive and wild populations.

## Other

Parvovirus, corona viruses. Tapeworms and other parasites.

## Research

Disease monitoring ongoing to protect wild populations from emerging threats.
