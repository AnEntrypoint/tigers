# Scientific Research

## Key Studies

- Genome sequencing studies (2018, 2021, 2023) clarified subspecies relationships
- Camera trap surveys since 2006 for population estimation
- Movement ecology studies using GPS collars

## Research Areas

Genetics, population dynamics, movement ecology, prey relationships, disease, climate impacts.

## Findings

Two-subspecies genetic model supported. 2018 genome study confirmed 6 living subspecies as distinct clades.
