# Population Monitoring

## Methods

- Camera trap surveys with individual identification
- DNA analysis from hair and scat samples
- Satellite/GPS collar tracking
- Sign surveys (tracks, scratches)

## Technology

Automated species identification from photographs using machine learning. Remote camera networks.

## Frequency

Comprehensive surveys every 4-6 years. Annual counts in key areas.

## Coordination

Global Tiger Initiative coordinates monitoring. Range state governments conduct national surveys.
