# Cub Rearing

## Development

- Birth: blind, helpless
- 2 weeks: eyes open
- 6 weeks: emerge from den
- 2 months: begin eating meat
- 3 months: follow mother to hunts
- 6 months: begin hunting lessons

## Weaning

Milk feeding 3-6 months. Meat consumption increases with age. Hunting skills develop through play and observation.

## Survival

50-70% mortality in first year. Causes: starvation, predation, disease, infanticide by rival males.

## Independence

Dispersal at 2-2.5 years. Cubs must establish own territory. High mortality during this transition.
