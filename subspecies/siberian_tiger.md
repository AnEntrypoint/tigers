# Siberian Tiger (Panthera tigris altaica)

## Description

Also known as Amur tiger. The largest surviving subspecies, adapted to extreme cold.

## Physical Features

Pale orange coat with thick winter fur in winter. Largest subspecies: males up to 320 kg, 3.7 m length.

## Population

Approximately 500-600 individuals, primarily in Russia's Sikhote-Alin mountains, with small numbers in China.

## Habitat

Temperate forests, birch forests, snowy conditions. Withstands temperatures to -40°C.

## Conservation

Protected in Sikhote-Alin and Land of the Leopard National Parks. Cross-border cooperation with China essential.
