# Extinct Tiger Subspecies

## Bali Tiger (Panthera tigris balica)

The smallest subspecies. Endemic to Bali. Extinct by ~1930s. Last confirmed specimen shot 1937.

## Javan Tiger (Panthera tigris sondaica)

Native to Java. Extinct by 1980s. Primary causes: habitat loss, hunting pressure.

## Caspian Tiger (Panthera tigris virgata)

Formerly ranged Turkey to Central Asia. One of the largest subspecies. Extinct by ~1970s.

## Causes of Extinction

- Unsustainable hunting
- Habitat destruction
- Prey depletion
- Human-tiger conflict
- No effective protection
