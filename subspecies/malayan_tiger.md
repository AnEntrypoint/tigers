# Malayan Tiger (Panthera tigris jacksoni)

## Description

Named after Australian zoologist Peter Jackson. Recognized as distinct subspecies in 2004 based on genetic studies.

## Physical Features

Similar to Indochinese tiger. Males weigh 120-190 kg. Coat slightly more orange than Indochinese.

## Population

~250 individuals in Peninsula Malaysia and southern Thailand.

## Habitat

Tropical rainforest, montane forest. Endemic to Malay Peninsula.

## Conservation

Mainly protected in Taman Negara and Endau-Rompin. Habitat fragmentation is major concern.
