Tiger Subspecies Overview

Recognized Subspecies (6 Living, 3 Extinct)

Living

  • Bengal (P. t. tigris): ~3,500 individuals
  • Siberian/Amur (P. t. altaica): ~500-600
  • Sumatran (P. t. sumatrae): ~400
  • Indochinese (P. t. corbetti): ~350
  • Malayan (P. t. jacksoni): ~250
  • South China (P. t. amoyensis): possibly extinct in wild

Extinct

  • Bali (P. t. balica): extinct ~1930s
  • Javan (P. t. sondaica): extinct ~1980s
  • Caspian (P. t. virgata): extinct ~1970s

Conservation Status

All living subspecies are endangered or critically endangered.
