# Communication

## Vocalizations

- **Roar**: Deep, resonant call carrying up to 3 km. Signals territory, locates mates, intimidates rivals. Only big cats with fully developed vocal apparatus can roar.
- **Chuff**: Soft, puffing sound used for friendly greetings between tigers, especially between mother and cubs.
- **Grunt**: Mild threat or warning to intruders.
- **Hiss/Growl**: Aggressive defensive sounds when cornered or protecting kill.
- **Mew**: High-pitched distress call from cubs.

## Visual Signals

Ear positions communicate aggression (ears flat) or confidence (ears forward). Tail position, bared teeth, and piloerection (fluffed fur) signal emotional state.
