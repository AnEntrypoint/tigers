Conservation Status

IUCN Red List

Panthera tigris: Endangered (EN)

  • Assessed: 2022
  • Criteria: A2bcd + 3cd

Population trend: Declining. No subspecies currently separately classified on IUCN Red List.

Major Threats

  • Habitat loss and fragmentation
  • Poaching for illegal trade
  • Human-wildlife conflict
  • Prey depletion

Legal Protection

CITES Appendix I. National legal protection in all range states. hunting prohibited.
