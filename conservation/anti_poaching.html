Anti-Poaching Measures

Patrols

Ranger teams conduct daily patrols. Armed response capability. Modern equipment.

Technology

Camera traps for detection. Drones in select areas. Radio communication networks.

Intelligence

Informant networks. Cross-border cooperation with neighboring countries.

Community Engagement

Local employment in conservation. Education programs. Alternative livelihoods reducing poverty-driven poaching.

Effectiveness

Mixed results — some areas showing reduced poaching, others facing increasing pressure.
