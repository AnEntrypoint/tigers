Threats: Human-Tiger Conflict

Causes

Livestock predation (goats, cattle). Prey depletion forces tigers toward domestic animals. Human encroachment into tiger habitat.

Retaliation

Killing tigers to protect livestock. Capture and translocation often fatal. Poisoning at kills.

Prevention

  • Livestock guard animals (dogs)
  • Improved enclosures
  • Compensation programs
  • Herd management practices

Programs

Community-based conservation shows 80%+ reduction in conflicts in some areas.
