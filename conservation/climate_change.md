# Threats: Climate Change

## Effects

Shifting prey distributions. Altered vegetation patterns. Changed disease dynamics. Sea level rise in coastal habitats (Sunderbans).

## Projections

Prey species may decline in some areas. Tigers may shift ranges to higher altitudes or latitudes.

## Adaptation Needs

Maintaining connectivity critical for range shifts. Protecting movement corridors essential.

## Research

Ongoing studies on climate impacts to tiger populations and ecosystems.
