# Captive Breeding

## Programs

Global Tiger Recovery Program coordinates captive breeding. Studbooks maintain genetic records across zoos.

## Major Programs

India: ~50 zoos with breeding programs.
China: South China tiger breeding program.
Global: SSP (Species Survival Plan) for each subspecies.

## Issues

Genetic bottlenecks in some subspecies. Inbreeding depression risk. Reintroduction training needed.

## Release Strategies

Captive-bred tigers require survival training. Soft release approaches with gradually increased freedom.
