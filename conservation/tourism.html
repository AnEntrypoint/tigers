Tiger Tourism

Ecotourism

Safari-based tourism generates significant revenue. Jeeps, guides in protected areas. Limited permits.

Destinations

India: Ranthambore most famous (book months in advance). Kaziranga, Bandhavgarh. Malaysia: Taman Negara.

Economic Benefits

Jobs, local businesses, community compensation funding. Funds conservation activities.

Risks

Disturbance if poorly managed. Minimum distance from tigers required. Carry capacity limits.
