# Success Stories

## Bengal Tigers

India's protected areas showing recovery. Ranthambore population increased from near-extirpation to ~50+ tigers.

## Siberian Tigers

Russia's protection efforts led to population stabilization and slight increase since 1990s.

## Global Trend

Population increased from ~3,000 (2010) to ~4,000 (2023) due to concentrated conservation.

## Conservation Lessons

Political will essential. Community involvement key. Adequate funding needed.Anti-poaching effective.
