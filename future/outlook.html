Future Outlook

Scenarios

Best Case: Populations stabilize with intensive conservation. Corridors connect habitats. Reintroduction succeeds.

Worst Case: Further extinctions. South China tiger lost. Genetic inbreeding crisis.

Most Likely: Gradual stabilization. Some local extinctions continue. Continued decline without major intervention.

Key Uncertainties

Climate change impacts. Poaching trends. Conservation funding. Political will.
