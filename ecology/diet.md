# Diet

## Primary Prey

Large ungulates form the bulk of diet:
- Sambar deer (most important)
- Chital/spotted deer
- Barasingha/swamp deer
- Wild boar
- Gaur/Indian bison

## Secondary Prey

Smaller mammals: monkeys, porcupines, hares. Birds, fish, insects when larger prey unavailable.

## Feeding Behavior

Consume 4-7 kg per meal. Can eat up to 20 kg in one sitting. May drag kill to cover, return over several days.

## Nutritional Needs

Require high-protein, high-fat meat. Consume approximately 15-20% of body weight in meat weekly.
