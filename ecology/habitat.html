Habitat

Primary Habitats

  • Tropical Forests: Dense vegetation in India, Southeast Asia. Consistent year-round prey.
  • Temperate Forests: Cold-adapted in Russian Far East. Seasonal prey availability.
  • Savanna: Grassland with scattered trees in India. Open hunting grounds.
  • Mangroves: Tidal wetlands (Sunderbans). Unique adaptation to tidal hunting.
  • Montane: High elevationsupto 3,000m in Himalayas.

Habitat Requirements

Water source required. Dense cover for stalking. Adequate prey base. Minimal human disturbance.

Adaptation

Each subspecies adapted to local conditions: Siberian = cold tolerance; Sumatran = forest agility; Bengal = heat tolerance and water access.
