# Gestation

## Duration

93-112 days (average 103 days). Slight variation by subspecies.

## Litter Size

2-4 cubs typical. Average 2-3. Single cub or quintuplets possible but rare.

## Birth

Cubs born blind, weighing 780-1,600 g. Eyes open at 6-14 days. Mostly helpless at birth.

## Den Sites

Caves, dense thick vegetation, rocky outcrops. Females select secluded locations, relocate cubs frequently.
