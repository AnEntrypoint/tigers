Mating Behavior

Season

Year-round in tropical regions. Winter mating (November-April) in temperate areas.

Courtship Signals

Females in estrus advertise through vocalizations and scent marks. Males locate females through scent, roam widely during this time.

Mating Process

Multiple matings over 4-6 day period. No pair bond forms; male departs after mating. Males may mate with multiple females in range.

Frequency

Females cycle every 3-9 weeks if not pregnant. Successful mating triggers false pregnancy in rare cases.
