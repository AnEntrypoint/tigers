  • Panthera tigris virgata — Caspian tiger (extinct ~1970s)

Taxonomic History

The tiger was first described by Carl Linnaeus in 1758 in Systema Naturae. Up to nine subspecies have been proposed, though recent genetic studies suggest only two distinct clades: continental tigers (P. t. tigris) and Sunda tigers (P. t. sondaica).

The IUCN Cat Specialist Group continues to review subspecies classification.
